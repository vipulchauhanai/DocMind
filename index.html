Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data completely secure? +
Yes, absolutely. DocMind is designed to run completely offline. Once installed, it does not send any files, prompts, metadata, or queries to remote servers. All vector indexing and model inferencing occur on your CPU/GPU directly.
What are the minimum system requirements? +
To run the AI models locally, we recommend:
  • OS: Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
  • RAM: 8 GB minimum (16 GB highly recommended)
  • Processor: Modern Intel Core i5/AMD Ryzen 5 or better
  • Storage: SSD highly recommended (~8 GB free space for full models)
How does the Online Installer work? +
The online installer is a lightweight wrapper. Upon launching, it scans your system for the Ollama engine, the BGE document AI model, and the Qwen Chat model. It only downloads the components you do not already have installed, saving you significant bandwidth.
Why is the offline ZIP package so large (6.5 GB)? +
To function completely offline, DocMind bundles the full weights for the Qwen Chat model (~1.8 GB) and the BGE-M3 text-embedding models (~2.2 GB), along with system installer files. Bundling everything ensures it can be installed on air-gapped computers or in places with no internet connection.
